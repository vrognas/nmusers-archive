About This Archive

What is NMusers?

NMusers is the primary mailing list for users of NONMEM, the gold-standard software for nonlinear mixed-effects modeling in pharmacometrics. The list has been active since 1995 and covers topics ranging from model coding and estimation methods to pharmacokinetic/pharmacodynamic theory.
