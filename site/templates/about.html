About This Archive

What is NMusers?

The NONMEM Users Network (NMusers) is the primary mailing list for users of NONMEM, the gold-standard software for nonlinear mixed-effects modeling in pharmacometrics.

It is maintained by ICON Clinical Research LLC and has been running since at least 1995. The list covers topics ranging from model coding and estimation methods to pharmacokinetic/pharmacodynamic theory.

NMusers has an RSS feed: maillist.xml

Subscribe

To subscribe to NMusers, send the following in the body (not the subject line) of an email to majordomo@globomaxnm.com:

subscribe nmusers

Alternatively, send a request to nmusers-request@iconplc.com.

Once subscribed, contribute to the discussion by emailing nmusers@globomaxnm.com.

Unsubscribe

Send the following in the body of an email to majordomo@globomaxnm.com:

unsubscribe nmusers

Posting Guidelines

  • Limit messages to 40,000 characters (including quoted reply history — truncate if too long)
  • No attachments
  • Only plain text and links

Search Tips

The search supports full-text queries across all archived messages. Tips:

  • Phrase search: "battle royale"
  • Boolean operators: +Jeff -Breidenbach, (dueling OR dualing) AND banjos
  • The search covers subject lines, author names, and message bodies.

Privacy

All messages in this archive were originally posted to a public mailing list. Email addresses have been stripped from the archive. Author names are shown as they appeared in the original messages.
